Nutrition for Everyday Healthy Life

Vitamins

Boost immunity and maintain overall health with essential vitamins like A, C, D, and E.

Minerals

Calcium, iron, and zinc help build strong bones and support vital body functions.

Proteins

Proteins repair tissues, build muscle, and keep you strong and active.

Carbohydrates

Carbs provide energy for your body and are vital for brain function.

Healthy Fats

Good fats like omega-3 support heart health and brain development.

Healthy Eating Tips

  • Eat a variety of colorful fruits and vegetables daily.
  • Drink at least 8 glasses of water.
  • Choose whole grains over refined carbs.
  • Limit processed sugar and junk food.
  • Balance your meals with proteins, carbs, and fats.
